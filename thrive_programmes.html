Roots and Wings

For teens aged 13–18. Deep roots of faith and character — and the wings to launch into adulthood with confidence.

Roots and Wings is Thrive's programme for the younger teenager — the 13 to 18-year-old who is still in the middle of figuring out who they are, navigating peer pressure, parent relationships, and a world that moves too fast.

Where the Identity & Purpose Seminar gives young adults language for who they already are, Roots and Wings builds the foundations before the storm — so that by the time life gets complicated, there is something solid to return to.

  • Self-awareness and emotional intelligence — understanding what you feel and why
  • Character development — integrity, resilience, empathy, and honesty in real-life scenarios
  • Parent-teen relationships — biblical frameworks for navigating the gap
  • Peer influence — how to stand firm when the crowd is pulling you somewhere else
  • Faith foundations — building a personal faith that isn't borrowed from your parents
  • Practical life skills — managing time, money, digital life, and relationships wisely

Thrive Bootcamp

An immersive faith-based residential experience. Bible study, leadership, service, and transformation — in three to five intense days.

The Thrive Bootcamp is the immersive version of everything Thrive stands for — a residential experience where young adults step fully out of their regular environment and into a concentrated season of growth, community, and deepened faith.

Unlike the online seminar, the bootcamp is built for those who want to go deeper — more time, more community, more space to wrestle with hard questions and come out the other side with clarity, conviction, and a community that holds them to it.

  • Daily Bible study and worship — not as programme, but as the fabric of every day
  • Team-building and leadership challenges — discovering who you are under pressure
  • Community service — experiencing the shift that comes from giving rather than acquiring
  • Identity, values, and purpose mapping — the full Thrive framework in an immersive format
  • Mentorship and small group conversations — the kind that change how you think
  • A community you'll carry into the rest of your life

Curriculum themes

What every Thrive
programme is built on.

Every Thrive programme — whether a 3-day seminar, a residential bootcamp, or a school talk — draws from the same curriculum foundation. These are the six themes that run through everything we do.

🪞
Identity & Self-Worth
Who you are — beyond labels, performance, and other people's expectations. How God's description of you predates every definition the world has tried to give you.
Psalm 139 1 Tim 4:12 Eph 2:10
⚖️
Values & Decision-Making
What you stand for — and how your values show up in real decisions before you consciously choose them. Building the decision filter that keeps you on track when life puts pressure on you.
Prov 4:23 Daniel 1:8 Matt 6:21
🧭
Purpose & Direction
Where you are going — not as a career plan but as a thread that runs through everything. How identity plus values equals direction. How your purpose was written before your confusion began.
Jer 29:11 Eph 2:10 Phil 1:6
🤝
Relationships & Community
How you treat people — parent-teen dynamics, peer influence, conflict resolution, hospitality, and the kind of community that makes growth possible. Including the B.R.I.D.G.E. and T.A.B.L.E. frameworks.
Matt 18:15 Col 3:13 Prov 27:17
💪
Character & Resilience
Who you become — integrity, perseverance, excellence, and the 5 A's for dealing with failure. How character is built in the small decisions nobody sees. Why becoming matters more than achieving.
1 Tim 4:12 Rom 8:28 Phil 4:13
🌿
Faith & Spiritual Formation
How you walk with God — building a personal faith through prayer, Scripture, and daily trust. The W.A.I.T., S.H.I.E.L.D., and R.E.S.T. frameworks for living a faith-rooted life in a noisy world.
Isa 40:31 Phil 4:6-7 Psalm 23
