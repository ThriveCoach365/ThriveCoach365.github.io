Why it matters

Not knowing who you are
is costing you something.

The problem isn't that you're lazy or lost. The problem is that nobody ever sat with you long enough to help you answer the questions that determine everything else. Thrive does that — in three days, with faith as the anchor and honesty as the method.

🪞
When you don't know who you are
You become what the room needs you to be. You shrink in some spaces and perform in others. You carry labels that were never yours. Thrive names that pattern — then helps you replace it with something true.
⚖️
When you don't know what you stand for
Every decision becomes negotiable. You drift into relationships, commitments, and directions that don't fit — because you have no internal compass to say "this isn't me." Thrive builds that compass.

What faith brings to the table
Scripture doesn't tell you who to become. It tells you who you already are. Thrive uses that as a foundation — not a lecture. You don't need to have your faith figured out. You just need to be open.

Why 3 days is enough
Because clarity isn't complicated — it's just rarely given the space it needs. Three focused sessions, honest work, and the right questions are enough to change how you see yourself. Then you carry that into every day after.

Questions

We've got answers.

What exactly will I walk away with? +
Three concrete, written deliverables: a personal identity statement (Day 1), your top 5 values articulated in your own words (Day 2), and a North Star purpose statement (Day 3). These are not abstract feelings — they are written documents you keep and return to. You also receive 3 workbooks, a 30-day email series, community access, and a certificate of completion.
Do I need to be religious or have a church background? +
No. Thrive is faith-rooted but not faith-exclusive. Our framework draws on Scripture because we believe it offers timeless wisdom on identity and purpose — but you don't need a church background to benefit. You don't need to have your faith figured out. We share these truths with warmth, not pressure, and every participant is welcomed exactly where they are.
Is this all theory or will it be practical? +
Every session is built around the principle: no theory without a tool. We never lecture for more than 15 minutes before moving into a solo exercise, peer breakout, or workbook activity. The sessions are designed to feel like a workshop, not a class. By the end of each day you have something written — not just something heard.
What happens after the seminar ends? +
The work doesn't stop on Day 3. You receive a 30-day post-seminar email series designed to keep you anchored to what you discovered. You also stay connected through the Thrive community. For those who want to go deeper, 1-on-1 coaching is available after the seminar — just indicate your interest on your Post-Session Reflection form.
Why is there a commitment fee rather than a free registration? +
Because we've found that people who invest — even a small amount — show up differently. The KSh 500 early bird fee is not about the money; it's about intention. It's your way of telling yourself: I'm serious about this. The fee is also fully credited toward any future Thrive programme.
What if I can't make one of the live sessions? +
We strongly encourage attending live — the community experience is central to how Thrive works. If you have a scheduling conflict, reach out to us before registering and we'll do our best to find a solution. Each session builds on the previous one, so attendance across all 3 days makes the biggest difference.
What do I need to participate? +
A phone or laptop with reliable internet, a quiet space where you can speak and listen freely, something to write with, and an open heart. We handle everything else. Sessions are designed to work even on a phone with limited data.
