Questions

We've got answers.

Who is this seminar for? +
Thrive is designed for teens and young adults aged 13–25 who are ready to do serious work on understanding who they are, what they believe, and where they're headed. Whether you're in a great place or a really hard one — you belong here.
Do I need to be a Christian to attend? +
Our framework is faith-rooted and Bible-based, but we welcome young people at any point of their spiritual journey. You don't need to have it all figured out. We do believe Scripture offers timeless wisdom — and we share it with warmth, not pressure.
What if I can't make one of the live sessions? +
We strongly encourage attending live — the community experience is a huge part of what makes Thrive work. That said, reach out to our team if you have a clash and we'll do our best to support you.
My parents want to know — is this safe? +
Absolutely. Thrive is a moderated, safe environment. Our facilitators are experienced mentors and every participant is treated with respect and care. Parents are welcome to reach out to us directly with any questions.
What do I need to participate? +
Just a phone or laptop with internet access, a quiet space, something to write with, and an open heart. We handle the rest.
