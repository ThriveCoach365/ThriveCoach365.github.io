The itch

You're busy.
But are you going anywhere?

  • You can describe what you do — but strip that away and you're not sure what's underneath
  • You say yes to things that don't feel right — and can't explain why they don't
  • You're moving, but not toward anything you consciously chose
  • You look around the room and wonder who to talk to
  • It feels like everyone else has it figured out except you
Thrive exists because these questions deserve a real answer — not a motivational quote, but honest work in a safe space, with someone who has lived the same itch.
What we do

Four ways Thrive walks
with young people.

Thrive uses four core approaches — each designed to meet young people where they are and move them toward who they're made to be.

🤝
Relational Mentoring
Building meaningful one-on-one relationships between mentors and mentees. Trust, open communication, and genuine presence — because transformation happens in relationship, not in programmes.
💪
Character Development
Interactive workshops on integrity, resilience, empathy, and decision-making — grounded in the realities of everyday teen and young adult life. Not theory. Practical tools you use on Monday.
🏕️
Faith-Based Boot Camps
Immersive residential experiences centred on building faith, leadership, and community service. Where participants deepen their understanding of their faith and its application to everyday life.
✦
Mentorship Seminars
Structured online seminars like the Identity & Purpose Seminar — three days, three deliverables, one direction that is finally yours. Faith-rooted, practical, and built so participants leave with something written.
📖
Resources & Content
Articles, Bible promises, weekly formulae, and free content for young people navigating identity, purpose, failure, and faith — written honestly from lived experience.
🌱
Community & Alumni
Every Thrive programme builds community — a group of peers who've done the same honest work and will hold each other to it. The community continues long after the final session ends.
The community you build here lasts.
Real people. Real transformation.
This is what after looks like.

Your facilitator

Meet Janet Oyende-Kariuki.

Janet has been working with young people for over a decade — in schools, universities, churches, and communities across Kenya and beyond. She is a learning and development professional, a missionary, a youth leader, and the person who built Thrive out of her own lived experience.

"I didn't always have the answers. As a teenager, I looked around the room and wondered who would reach out. That memory is why Thrive exists."

She visits high school and university campuses as a speaker, serves on the board of Generation Youth Christ Africa, and leads youth ministry at her local church. She is still in the rooms where young people are.
